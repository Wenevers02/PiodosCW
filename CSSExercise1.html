BLACKPINK becomes 1st K-pop act to headline Coachella

BLACKPINK made their Coachella debut in 2019, but only as a performer and not a headliner. This year will mark their return to the Coachella stage in four years. BLACKPINK debuted in 2016 under YG Entertainment. Composed of Jennie, Jisoo, Lisa, and Rose.
